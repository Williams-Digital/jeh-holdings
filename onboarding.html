Client Onboarding

Welcome to Williams Digital

Thank you for choosing us as your digital partner. This onboarding guide will walk you through the process and help us gather everything we need to bring your vision to life.

01

Project Overview & Timeline

Now that the agreement is signed, here's what to expect. We follow a structured process to ensure your website is built efficiently and to the highest standard.

  • Phase 1 — Discovery & Planning We'll review your questionnaire responses, discuss your goals, and define the project scope. This is where we align on the direction for your website.
  • Phase 2 — Design You'll receive design concepts and mockups for review. This is your opportunity to provide feedback and request revisions before we move into development.
  • Phase 3 — Development We build your website with clean, responsive code. You'll receive progress updates and a staging link to preview the site as it comes together.
  • Phase 4 — Review & Launch You'll have 5 business days to review the completed site and provide final feedback. Once approved, we go live.

Communication

We'll primarily communicate via email. You can expect updates at each phase milestone. Please designate one point of contact to streamline feedback and approvals.

Turnaround

Response times from the client directly affect the project timeline. We ask that all feedback and materials be provided within 4 business days of each request to keep things moving smoothly.

02

Business Information

Help us understand your business so we can tailor the website to your brand and audience.

Company Name

What is the full legal name of your business?

Industry & Services

What industry are you in and what services or products do you offer?

Target Audience

Who are your ideal clients or customers? Describe their demographics, needs, and how they typically find you.

Mission & Vision

What is your company's mission statement or core values? What message do you want your website to convey?

Unique Value Proposition

What sets you apart from competitors? Why should someone choose you over the alternatives?

03

Brand & Design Preferences

Understanding your visual preferences helps us design a website that truly represents your brand.

Existing Branding

Do you have an existing logo, brand colors, or fonts? If so, please provide the files and any brand guidelines you may have.

Color Preferences

What colors do you want your website to use? Are there any colors you specifically want to avoid?

Style & Mood

How would you describe the look and feel you're going for? (e.g., modern and minimal, bold and corporate, warm and approachable, dark and luxurious)

Inspiration Sites

Share 2-3 websites you admire. What do you like about them? This helps us understand your aesthetic preferences.

Competitors

Who are your main competitors? Share their websites so we can understand your market and ensure your site stands out.

04

Website Requirements

Let us know what you need from your website so we can plan the right structure and features.

Pages Needed

What pages do you need? (e.g., Home, About, Services, Contact, Blog, FAQ, Team, Portfolio, Pricing)

Features & Functionality

Are there specific features you need? (e.g., contact form, appointment booking, newsletter signup, social media feeds, client portal, payment integration)

Call to Action

What is the primary action you want visitors to take on your website? (e.g., call you, fill out a form, schedule a consultation, make a purchase)

Existing Website

Do you have a current website? If so, what do you like and dislike about it? What would you like to keep or change?

05

Content & Assets

Great content is the foundation of a great website. Let us know what you already have and what you'll need help with.

Written Content

Do you have written content (copy) ready for the website, or will you need assistance with copywriting?

Photos & Images

Do you have professional photos of your team, office, or products? If not, are you open to using high-quality stock photography?

Logo Files

Please provide your logo in high-resolution format (PNG, SVG, or AI preferred). If you need a logo designed, let us know.

Social Media

List all your social media profiles and any other online presence you'd like linked from the website.

Testimonials & Reviews

Do you have client testimonials, case studies, or reviews you'd like featured on the website?

06

Domain & Hosting

We'll handle the technical setup — just let us know where things currently stand.

Domain Name

Do you already own a domain name? If so, what is it and where is it registered (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains)?

Preferred Domain

If you don't have a domain, what domain name would you like? Provide a few options in case your first choice is unavailable.

Email

Do you have a professional email address (e.g., info@yourcompany.com)? Would you like us to set one up for you?

07

Account Access

To set up and manage your website, we may need access to the following accounts. Please share credentials securely — never send passwords in plain email.

Secure Sharing

We recommend sharing credentials via a secure method such as a password manager sharing feature or an encrypted message. We will provide instructions on how to share securely.

  • Domain registrar — login to manage DNS settings
  • Current hosting provider — if migrating an existing site
  • Google Analytics — if you have an existing account
  • Google Business Profile — for local SEO management
  • Social media accounts — if integration or linking is needed
  • Email provider — if connecting professional email
08

Goals & Success Metrics

Understanding what success looks like for you helps us build a website that delivers real results.

Primary Goal

What is the #1 thing you want your website to accomplish? (e.g., generate leads, build credibility, sell products, inform clients)

Success Metrics

How will you measure the website's success? (e.g., number of inquiries, page views, conversion rate, search ranking)

SEO Goals

Are there specific keywords or phrases you'd like your website to rank for in search engines?

Future Plans

Are there features or pages you'd like to add in the future but don't need right now? This helps us build a flexible foundation.

09

Next Steps

To get started, please prepare the following items. You don't need everything at once — we'll work through these together.

  • Complete the questionnaire sections above
  • Gather your logo files (PNG, SVG, or AI format)
  • Collect any photos or images you'd like to use
  • Prepare written content for key pages (or let us know if you need help)
  • List 2-3 websites you admire for design inspiration
  • Confirm your domain name and registrar details
  • Gather any account credentials to share securely
  • Submit your 50% deposit to begin the project
